The goal of this project is to help you optimize your intagram hashtag game. Instagram hashtags categorize your post, but also can help your content reach a wider audience. For companies that have an instagram presence, or hire instagram influencers to promote their products, making sure each post reaches as wide an audience as possible is crucial. Improving the content of your posts is difficult, but changing out your hashtags for ones that will increase viewrship is now easy! Just put in the hashtags you want to use, and the recommender will suggest alternative hashtags which are thematically similar, but happen to be more popular according to our analysis.
